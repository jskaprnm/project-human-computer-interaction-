About Us


Hi-Toyz is a toy store that has been around since 2019. Hi-Toyz is a store that sells toys like figures, statues, and model kits that are made of plastic. Hi-Toyz is highly appreciated by the collectors of the area. There are many different brand name toys available at Hi-Toyz for purchase. Our store now has the permission to offer popular brands such as Mattel Toys, MiniGT, Bandai, and Tamiya to customers.
